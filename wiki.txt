This program was created by George Hatzis-Schoch for the class Humanoids taught by Christopher Atkeson at Carnegie Mellon. 

To enable voice control, press triangle.

To exit voice control, say exit, stop, or quit.

To place a block, press X button.

To place a structure, press O button.

To rotate a block press L1 and R1.

Use the arrow keys to move the block location.
